Skype


Skype Lessons allow you to get world class guitar instruction from the comfort of your own home. Here's what you need to get started:

1) A computer with a webcam and an internet connection
2) Download the free Skype online video chat service here. Make sure to make an account!
3) Contact me via email or phone and set up your first lesson. Once we've arranged a lesson time, confirm your lesson time by purchasing your first lesson using the button below.
4) Sign on a few minutes before your lesson time and add me GianGuitar to your contact list
5) Lesson time! The lesson proceeds like normal from here, all handouts, song pdfs and backing tracks will be emailed to you.

FAQ

Why should I take lessons with you?
For starters, I'm unconventional. I did not like the normal studies offered and have instead taken it upon myself to create a curriculum that suits the needs of eccentric individuals and eager learners.

What are your rates?
Rates vary,please inquiry through email or text.

How do I book Gian to teach me?
Please head to the contact page and get in touch with Gian.

How long are the sessions?
Sessions last anywhere between 45 mins. to 1 hr.

When do you teach?
Depends on availability of student but I prefer Saturday & Sunday mornings.

Where are the lessons held?
I teach at my home. In my special mancave (garage) wannabe studio. You'll love it!

Do you offer a video curriculum?
Video curriculum/lessons coming soon. Stay tuned!

Do you do in-home lessons or group lessons?
I prefer to teach in the comfort of my home but I can def. consider different circumstances. Group lessons all good!

Will I learn the same things if I take Skype Lessons instead of in-person?
If you ask me, it really all depends on how the student wants to learn. We all learn in different ways. Location is also a huge factor in deciding whether the student wants to learn through Skype or in-person.

I have a unique schedule. Can we work around it?
As I mention above, I am def. willing to consider options. My main concern is the clarity of the lesson. Late nights, no rest, no sleep, or teaching at odd hours is not beneficial for anyone.

What is the age range?
10 and up.

Do you offer gift certificates?
Gift Certicates coming soon.

How many lessons in a week?
1 lesson per week.

How long until I start to rock?
Remember future rock star, Rome was not built overnight. Learning how to play guitar cannot be learned overnight. Practice always and never stop learning.
