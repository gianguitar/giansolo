FAQ/Lesson Policy

LESSONS POLICY

Lessons are sold on a monthly basis. In the 1st session, the student and instructor meet and greet and decide at end of discussions whether its a good fit for both and move forward. All lessons are charged prior to the lesson. Purchased lessons expire 4 months from the date of purchase unless plans have been made with instructor prior to expiration.

The student will promise to practice at their scheduled practice time and not divert or waiver from the rule. The student is expected to follow and practice correctly and accurately for maximum learning. The proposed practice time is 45 mins. Practice should be 2-4 times a week to maximize learning and see results in less time. Practice is key to learning how to play the guitar progress beyond your wildest guitar dreams so don't pay for lessons if this is not the standard that you are hoping to achive.

All Skype lessons, sessions and consultations are subject to a 48-hour cancellation policy. If you cannot make it to your scheduled lesson, session or consultation, you must provide a 48-hour cancellation notice prior or get in contact to reschedule for another time. No notice of cancellation results in a full charge as a no-show fee and services rendered. There are no exceptions to this rule. Being late to the lesson also does not guarantee you extra time at the end of your scheduled time, not unless deemed necessary by the instructor.
